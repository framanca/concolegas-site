Privacy Policy – Concolegas EN

Last updated: 2026-01-18

Concolegas (the “app”) is a party games and scoreboards app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it’s used, and your choices.

1) Controller and contact

  • Controller: Concolegas
  • Support contact: soporte@concolegas.com
  • Privacy contact: info@concolegas.com

2) Data we process

2.1 Data you provide

  • Emails or messages you send to info@concolegas.com or soporte@concolegas.com (message content and your email), used to reply and provide support.

2.2 Data collected automatically

  • Technical and usage data (e.g., device model, OS version, language, time zone, usage events, diagnostic data).
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / IDFA when applicable) and related data to serve ads and measure performance.
  • Crash logs to improve stability and performance.

2.3 In-app content

  • Games, settings, and scoreboards are typically stored on your device.
  • If you use online/multiplayer features, some gameplay data may be processed to provide the service (e.g., room code, game state, player names/avatars if enabled).

3) How we use data

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and understand feature usage.
  • Show ads and measure ad performance (if the app displays ads).
  • Respond to support requests.

4) Advertising (AdMob and other networks)

The app may display ads using Google AdMob and/or other partners. These partners may use advertising identifiers and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads.
  • Limit frequency.
  • Measure performance.
  • Prevent fraud.

You can manage ad personalization and tracking in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple
