Privacy Policy – Concolegas EN

Last updated: 2026-01-18

Concolegas (the “app”) is a party games and scoreboards app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your options.

1) Controller and contact

  • Controller: Concolegas
  • Support and privacy contact: info@concolegas.com

2) Data we process

2.1 Data you provide to us

  • Emails or messages you send to info@concolegas.com (message content and your email address), used to respond and provide support.

2.2 Data collected automatically

  • Technical and usage data (for example: device model, operating system version, language, time zone, usage events, diagnostics data).
  • Advertising identifiers (for example, Advertising ID / IDFA where applicable) and related data to show ads and measure performance.
  • Crash logs to improve stability and performance.

2.3 In-app content

  • Games, settings, and scoreboards are typically stored on your device.
  • If you use online/multiplayer features, some game information may be processed to provide the service (for example: room code, game state, names/avatars if you enable them).

3) How we use data

  • Provide and maintain the app’s functionality.
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and understand how features are used.
  • Show ads and measure their performance (if the app displays advertising).
  • Respond to support requests.

4) Advertising (AdMob and other networks)

The app may show ads using Google AdMob and/or other partners. These partners may use advertising identifiers and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads.
  • Limit frequency.
  • Measure performance.
  • Prevent fraud.

You can manage ad personalization and tracking from your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising (and tracking permission if applicable).
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads (or Privacy > Ads, depending on your version).

5) Third parties

Depending on the enabled features, the app may integrate third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) for ads.
  • Firebase (Google LLC) for online features, analytics, or diagnostics (if enabled).

These providers may process data under their own privacy policies.

6) Legal basis

Where applicable (for example, in the EU), we may rely on:

  • Legitimate interest to maintain and improve the app (diagnostics and security).
  • Performance of a contract when you use online features.
  • Consent when required (for example, personalized ads or tracking on iOS where applicable).

7) Data retention

  • Support emails: as long as needed to handle your request and comply with legal obligations.
  • Technical/diagnostics data: only as long as necessary to improve the app and security, depending on provider configuration and settings.

8) Children’s privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and may be used by families. If you are a parent/guardian and believe a child has sent us personal data, email us at info@concolegas.com and we will handle it.

9) Your rights

Where applicable (for example, GDPR in the EU), you may request access, rectification, deletion, objection, restriction, or portability when relevant. To exercise these rights, email info@concolegas.com and describe your request.

10) Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information. No method is 100% secure.

11) Changes

We may update this policy. We will publish the updated version and the “Last updated” date.

12) Contact

Support and privacy questions: info@concolegas.com
